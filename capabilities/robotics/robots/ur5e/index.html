    Robots UR5e

    UR5e Home

    The Universal Robots UR5e is a lightweight, 6-axis collaborative robotic arm (cobot) designed to work safely alongside humans.

    Quick Links

    • Usage Log: All activity on the UR5e must be logged. Follow this link to update the log.
    • Machine Calendar: Book time with the UR5e or see when it's available. Users with reservations will have priority access.

    Getting Started

    Before using the UR5e, you must complete hands-on training. Schedule time here or contact Ryan Kuederle.

    After completing training, you can reference the UR5e Standard Operating Procedures during regular use. Guides for advanced features will be made available in the future.
